Brand Identity Design

Logos, colors, and guidelines that make your business recognizable everywhere.

Create a Brand That Sticks

A strong identity is more than a logo. It’s a system — logos, color, typography, imagery, and rules — that communicates who you are with clarity and consistency.

We craft clean, modern identities that scale from social media avatars to vehicle wraps. You’ll get production-ready files and a practical brand book your team can follow.

What's Included

Logo System

Primary + alternate marks, light/dark versions, and clear-space rules.

Typography

Headline/body font pairings with web-safe fallbacks and usage rules.

Color Palette

Primary/secondary colors with HEX, RGB, and CMYK values.

Brand Guidelines

Practical PDF brand book for consistent use across print & digital.

Our Process

01
Discovery

Workshop + research to understand your audience, competitors, and goals.

02
Concepts

Present multiple directions; iterate based on feedback to a chosen route.

03
System Build

Finalize logos, color, type, and visual language for all applications.

04
Guidelines & Handover

Deliver files + brand book so your team and partners stay on-brand.

  • Revisions:Up to 3 rounds
  • Deliverables:Logos, palette, type, brand book (PDF)
  • File Formats:AI, PDF, SVG, PNG
