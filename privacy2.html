Privacy Policy

How MetroSites collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: September 24, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or services, we may collect:

  • Personal details (name, email, phone) when you fill out forms.
  • Business/project information you share with us.
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and usage analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to enquiries and provide requested services.
  • Prepare proposals, contracts, and invoices.
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience.
  • Send important updates, offers, or communications (with your consent).

3. Data Protection

We take appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no online transmission is 100% secure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted third parties (e.g., hosting providers, payment processors) strictly for delivering services.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and third-party analytics tools (like Google Analytics) to understand visitor behavior and improve performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us directly.

7. Retention

We retain client and project data only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
